## 🧠 2. Advanced Ensemble Learning Suite
*Core focus: Boosting and Bagging techniques to minimize Bias and Variance.*

### 🩺 Breast Cancer Diagnostic Classification 🎀
* **Goal:** Maximize recall for malignant tumor detection.
* **Techniques:** **Random Forest** (Bagging) to reduce variance and **AdaBoost/XGBoost** to correct sequential errors.
* **Impact:** Achieves high sensitivity, reducing the risk of false negatives in clinical diagnostics.

### 🏠 California Housing Price Regression 🌴
* **Goal:** Predict median house values across complex spatial districts.
* **Techniques:** **XGBoost** and **CatBoost** for non-linear data; **Model Stacking** to combine multiple base learners.
* **Impact:** Optimized for RMSE and $R^2$ to provide accurate real-estate market valuations.
